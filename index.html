About us

Welcome, We are Al Mokhtar Travel Agency and our only purpose is to ensure a Visa Application does not stop you from achieving your dream of traveling the world.

With more than 5 years of experience, the Al Mokhtar Travel Agency team has had thousands of travel and tourism service requests successfully completed and received many thanks, photos and even postcards from our valued, happy clients who have been able to travel all over the world.
